WELCOME TO MY CORNER OF THE INTERNET!

I'm Madeline but you can call me Maddie. There is not much to know about me I'm just a French dreamer, living in the middle of nowhere. I haven't figured out exactly where I'm going in life, but here I am.
I'm going to share the things that matter to me. First, you need to know is that I'm a travel lover, I love to meet new people learn things about them and their culture. To me traveling is a good way to learn about yourself, we have this proverb in France: "Les voyages formes la jeunesse" which can be translated by "Travel broadens the mind" I find it truthful and meaningful. During these trips, I like to capture moments through pictures.
By the way, as you might understand I love the sea.
